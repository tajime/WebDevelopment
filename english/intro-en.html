School introduction

School itinerary

Our school was renovated from a Japanese school in London.
Until then, it was a school for Japanese people, but it was reborn 10 years ago as an international school
with the aim of developing international human resources.

Overview of our school

Our school is committed to developing human resources who can play an active role internationally
and have world-class management skills.
We support young people who are eager to learn, regardless of race or gate.

Development of management skills

The school offers unique curriculum and active training in order to develop rich management skills. We develop practical skills and raise your abilities to a world-class level.

International School

We aim to educate international students.
You usually speak English and various races, especially Japanese and French, study together in the same space.
You can spend three years with a lot of stimulation because of the environment where
there are multinational races from an early stage.
