Education content

Our school is also focusing on education. Some of the lessons are listed below.

Liberal arts

You study liberal arts subjects in all grades. Teachers particularly focuse on the first year, as it is the basis for the second and third year classes.English classes focus on communication, and sometimes involve off-campus learning. We aim to achieve a TOEIC score of 900 or higher.In mathematics lessons, students are asked to explain their homework and teach others to improve their understanding.

Law

You learn also about the law. Everything in the world is ruled by the law, so it's in a world with law. Anyway, it ’s huge and hard to remember,and we offer the following learning methods. You take a quiz at the beginning of each class and again the next week with the same range of quizzes. By using not only inputs but also outputs many times, you will have a firm understanding of the law.

Marketing

You learn about marketing, the basis of management. Marketing is "an activity that research the products and services consumers want, and smooth the distribution from producers to consumers by determining the products to be supplied and the method of sales activities (about marketing). The most important thing in marketing is to understand the market accurately. You may feel it difficult to study, but our teachers will explain with confidence.

Finance

After all, you have to learn finances. It is no exaggeration to say that money is what makes the world. First, you will learn from the history of the monetary system and the importance of money and the current monetary system. You also learn things that you do not usually handle such as stocks and exchanges, and aim to visualize the flow of money in the world. Let's learn how to turn the economy and be able to handle money well.

Programming

In today's information society, this is a very important lesson, as it is said that "programming ability is a person's ability". Even from a global perspective, programming classes have begun to be adopted. You start with basic computer operation, and it becomes a bit more difficult, but please be assured that our professional teachers will be attentive to your needs. Eventually, you will create your own homepage.

e-business

Have you ever heard the word e-business? E-business is "to completely digitize business processes by introducing the Internet and related information and communication technologies across all business activities of companies. And it's business activities and commerce,pass such a change and deeply digitized." (about e-business). You learn from the importance of e-business and the origin of e-business, and ultimately build a simple e-business mechanism.
